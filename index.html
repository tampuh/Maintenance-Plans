Maintenance Plans

Our maintenance plans are designed for clients who want their vehicle kept consistently clean without needing frequent full details.

These services are available after an initial full detail and focus on preserving the vehicle’s condition, not correcting heavy wear.

What’s Included

Exterior
  • Hand wash and dry
  • Wheel and tire cleaning
  • Tire dressing
  • Exterior glass cleaning
  • Light paint condition inspection
Interior
  • Light vacuum
  • Wipe-down of high-touch surfaces
  • Interior glass cleaning
  • Light dust and debris removal

Not Included (Add-Ons)

  • Pet hair removal
  • Stain removal
  • Heavy interior cleaning
  • Carpet shampooing or steam cleaning
  • Paint decontamination or correction

Add-ons are available if needed and priced separately.

Plan Options & Pricing

Monthly Maintenance

Ideal for daily drivers and consistently used vehicles.

Sedan: $50 / visit
SUV / Truck: $60 / visit

Service every 4–5 weeks

Quarterly Maintenance

Best for lighter-use vehicles or garage-kept cars.

Sedan: $150 / visit
SUV / Truck: $170 / visit

Service every 3 months

Important Notes
  • Initial full detail required before enrollment
  • Pricing assumes maintained condition
  • Excessive dirt or neglect may require add-ons
  • Plans can be adjusted or canceled anytime
